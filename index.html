How it works

Your browser downloads a test file from this site's own server across multiple parallel streams, measuring real sustained throughput. Ping is calculated from ten round-trips with statistical outlier trimming. No third-party servers are involved at any point.

Your privacy

This tool collects absolutely no data about you. No IP address, no location, no device information, no personal data of any kind. We operate no database and no logging system. There is nothing to consent to and nothing to opt out of.

Accuracy

Results are estimates reflecting conditions at the time of testing. For the most reliable reading, close other applications and browser tabs and use a wired connection where possible. Results are indicative only and should not be used as the sole basis for any dispute with your broadband provider.
